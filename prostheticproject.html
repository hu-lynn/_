Prosthetic Finger Project

2023 - NOW

Project Overview

My father lost his ring finger in 2017 resulting in difficulties in daily life (reduced strength in that hand and difficulty holding certain objects). To help not only him hopefully but millions of people across the world, my project aims to make a prosthetic which can perform the functions of a real finger in daily life, using a flex sensor and a motor.

Currently, I am researching into the motion of a finger, so that I can produce a realistic simulation of any fingers' movement without the need of another one.
