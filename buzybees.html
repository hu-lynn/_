Buzy Beez Study App

November 2024

Project Overview

An Android app in Kotlin to help support students with organising extracurriculars, clubs and activities. A lot of self-learning and tutorials and starting experience in UI/UX. Group projects are always a great way to learn and collaborate.

In this project, I coded the Login and Suggestions page and learned how to put everyone's code together into one project.
